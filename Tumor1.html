Tumor Segmentation

Our Tumor Segmentation model is designed to precisely identify and delineate tumors from medical images. Utilizing state-of-the-art deep learning techniques, this model assists radiologists in detecting tumors at an early stage, ensuring timely intervention and treatment.

Bone Fracture Detection

Bone fractures can be subtle and easily missed, but our Bone Fracture Detection model enhances diagnostic accuracy by automatically identifying fractures in X-ray images. This model is trained on a vast dataset of bone images, enabling it to recognize even the most minute fractures.

Lung Cancer Detection

Lung cancer is one of the leading causes of cancer-related deaths worldwide. Early detection is crucial for improving survival rates. Our Lung Cancer Detection model leverages advanced convolutional neural networks to analyze CT scans and detect early signs of lung cancer.

Focal Liver Classification

Liver diseases can manifest in various forms, and accurate classification is vital for effective treatment. Our Focal Liver Classification model specializes in distinguishing between different types of liver lesions, including benign and malignant tumors.
